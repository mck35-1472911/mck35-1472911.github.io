Features

Everything you get from hiking with us

  • Experienced Trail Guide

    Our trail guides will lead you on the best path and make sure that everyone is comfortable with the pace. They will ensure a great hike every time!

  • Assistance at every step of the way

    Our trail guides are equipped with frist aid kits, water filters, and other emergency supplies to be ready to react to any situation you may encounter.

  • Cars are eco friendly

    Don't worry about driving long distances, our fleet of cars are all electric to save the environment we love to hike in!

  • Find hikes easily

    With a easy search engine and filters for all types of hikes, we know that you will find the perfect hike to fit your needs.

  • All gear availiable

    Any gear you need is availiable to rent and you don't have to worry about picking it up or dropping it off anywhere.

  • Support the environment

    5% of all proceeds go directly back to preserving the environment that we hike in

  • Transportation to and from

    Pick ups from your home to the mountain and back you never have to worry about getting lost

  • Overnight hike availiable

    We can set up hike for overnight trips as well for when you want to take the next step over a quick day hike.

  • View the outdoors

    Get breathtaking views that you can't find anywhere else.
